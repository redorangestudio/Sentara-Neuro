  • Spine Neurosurgery

Expertise and Collaboration

Our surgeons have unique expertise, providing patients with the most advanced care possible. We take a unique team approach to care. Neurosurgeons work side by side with world-class specialists, neuropsychologists, nutritionists and physical therapists.

High-Tech Facilities

The Sentara Neurosciences program offers dedicated operating suites with the latest technology, a neurosciences ICU and a comprehensive surgical program. We focus on minimally invasive techniques using the newest technology. Sentara has the only intraoperative MRI in Hampton Roads. The iMRI allows for real-time imaging during surgery, which means better patient outcomes.

Research

Our leading-edge research, clinical trials, and patient-centered outcomes distinguish us. We are committed to improving the lives of patients and working toward breakthrough discoveries. Our doctors collaborate with Eastern Virginia Medical School and other specialists in diseases of the brain and spine.
